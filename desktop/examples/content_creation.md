# Content Creation Example

## Original Prompt
> "Write a blog post about AI"

## Optimized Prompt
> Act as a technology journalist who covers AI for a business audience.
>
> Write a 1,200-word blog post about how small businesses are using AI tools in 2026 to automate customer service.
>
> Target audience: Small business owners (10-50 employees) who are curious about AI but have no technical background.
>
> Structure:
> 1. Hook with a specific small business success story
> 2. 3-4 practical AI tools with use cases and pricing
> 3. Implementation steps a non-technical owner can follow
> 4. Common pitfalls and how to avoid them
> 5. Call to action
>
> Tone: Practical, encouraging, jargon-free. Use "you" to address the reader directly.

## What Changed
Added role prompting (tech journalist), specified audience (SMB owners), defined structure with 5 sections, set word count (1,200), and calibrated tone for non-technical readers. Decomposed the vague "AI" topic into a specific angle (customer service automation).

## Techniques Applied
- Role Prompting → Tech journalist
- User Persona → SMB owners, non-technical
- Specificity → Word count, structure, tone
- Decomposition → 5-section structure
