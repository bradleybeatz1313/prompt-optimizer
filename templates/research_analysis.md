# Research Analysis Template

```
<role>Act as a senior research analyst specializing in [DOMAIN].</role>

<context>
Research question: [QUESTION]
Scope: [SCOPE/CONSTRAINTS]
Prior knowledge: [WHAT WE ALREADY KNOW]
</context>

<instruction>
Conduct a structured analysis:

Step 1: Identify the key dimensions of this question
Step 2: For each dimension, analyze current evidence and trends
Step 3: Identify gaps, contradictions, or areas of uncertainty
Step 4: Synthesize findings into actionable recommendations

Format as a structured report with:
- Executive summary (3-5 sentences)
- Analysis by dimension (with supporting evidence)
- Risk factors and uncertainties
- Recommended next steps (prioritized)
</instruction>
```
